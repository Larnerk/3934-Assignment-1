My Recipe

This is my all-time favourite recipe for delicious chocolate chip cookies. Below is all the information necessary to make these cookies, all the way from the ingredients used to each step of the process. This recipe will take approximately twenty minutes total for prep time, and another 10 for cooking. Additionally, the cookies should take around ten minutes to bake, and a half hour to cool off, leaving your total time at one hour. There is about a 24 serving yield from this recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions:

  1. 1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. 2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. 3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
